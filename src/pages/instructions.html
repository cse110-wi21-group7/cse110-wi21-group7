Welcome to the Pomodero Technique!

History

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length and separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for 'tomato'!

It’s as easy as 1, 2, 3!

Step 1

Take some time to think about the time each of your tasks are going to require.

Step 2

After you press “Show Timer”, you’ll be asked to input the amount of time that you decided earlier and we’ll break down your work sessions into the appropriate pomodoro intervals!

Step 3

After you’re done with one full task you’ll see a popup that asks you to enter the amount of pomodoro intervals that task actually ended up taking!
